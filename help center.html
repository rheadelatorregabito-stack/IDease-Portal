Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about your student ID

How do I activate my student ID card?

To activate your student ID, visit the student services desk or follow the activation link sent to your university email. Activation typically takes a few minutes.

What should I do if I lost my student ID?

Report the loss immediately via the Submit Request form. A replacement fee may apply. Check the Replacement ID policy for details.

Can I use my student ID for campus dining?

Yes — once activated, your student ID may be used for dining services and on-campus purchases where accepted by the merchant.

How do I update my photo on the student ID?

Use the Submit Request form, choose 'Photo Update', and upload a recent passport-style photo. Follow the imaging guidelines for best results.

What access does my student ID provide?

Student IDs grant access to campus buildings, library services, and student discounts. Specific access rights are assigned by departments.

How do I check my student ID balance?

Check your balance through the student portal's account page or visit the campus card office for assistance.
